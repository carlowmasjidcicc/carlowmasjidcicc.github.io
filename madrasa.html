Children's Madrasa

Welcome to our Islamic Supplementary Evening School, where we offer comprehensive classes in Islamic Studies, Qur'an Recitation, and Qur'an Memorisation, all aligned with the authentic teachings of the Qur'an and the ways of the Prophet Muhammad. Our Madrasah aims to create a nurturing and secure Islamic environment, fulfilling the obligation of seeking knowledge while instilling excellent manners and nurturing future leaders within our community.

With a well-structured curriculum and a focus on promoting positive behavior through our Behaviour Policy and Rewarding System, we strive to cultivate a love for learning the deen (Islamic knowledge) and foster respect and care among all children. We value the vital partnership between the Madrasah and the home in shaping well-rounded and confident Muslim individuals, firmly believing that through collective efforts, we can provide our children with a unique and enriching education.

Please Note

Due to the current pandemic, we have reviewed the Government guidelines for educational out of School settings and drawn up our risk assessment accordingly. Kindly adhere to the new guidelines as listed below:

  • 1. If any child has any Covid related symptoms such as high temperature, dry cough or flu like symptoms then they should NOT attend Madrasa.
  • 2. Face masks are not mandatory, however if any parent wishes that their child should wear a mask then they may do so.
  • 3. Hand sanitisers will be available in the Madrasa.
  • 4. All parents should strictly ensure the children visit the washroom and complete wudhu before they attend Madrasa. th wudhu area and toilets will be out of bounds, unless in emergency.
  • 5. If your child has a medical issue then please inform the Imam/Apa as soon as possible.
  • 6. Children will be made to sit in a distance from each other to ensure social distancing.
  • 7. Parents dropping/picking children should avoid loitering and gathering around in the Masjid car park.
