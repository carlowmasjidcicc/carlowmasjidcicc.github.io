About the Campaign

The Carlow Islamic Cultural Centre (CICC) is a beacon of faith, serving the religious, cultural, and social needs of Muslims in Carlow and surrounding areas. Based in the heart of Carlow town in southern Ireland, the CICC is dedicated to enriching the lives of our community through two primary endeavours: the Carlow Mosque and our Islamic School. The Carlow Mosque is a sanctuary for worship and community activities, while our Islamic School offers comprehensive classes in Islamic Studies, Qur'an Recitation, and Qur'an Memorisation, all in line with the pure teachings of the Qur'an and the ways of the Prophet Muhammad (P.B.U.H).

Currently, our mosque and school operate in a rented facility with limited capacity, unable to accommodate the growing number of Muslims in our area. To address this pressing need, we are launching an urgent appeal to build a permanent Mosque and Islamic School. The Carlow Mosque and Islamic School Project aims to acquire a larger property to establish a lasting legacy of faith, community, and learning.

Driven by faith, unity, and a spirit of giving, this project aspires to foster peace and understanding across diverse communities. We implore you to spread our message and donate generously. Your support will help us build more than just a structure; it will help create a lasting testament of faith and community for generations to come. Your contribution is not just a donation—it is an investment in the heart of Islamic life and a path to immense spiritual rewards in this life and the hereafter.

Please join us in this noble endeavour. Donate now and be part of a legacy that will resonate with faith and community for years to come.
